Our Story

Born from a Grandmother's Kitchen

Golden Crumb began in a small home kitchen where every morning was blessed with the scent of fresh bread and warm chai. We've carried that spirit forward — baking not just treats, but memories.

Every recipe is tested by tradition, every ingredient sourced with care. We believe in slow-baked goodness that nourishes body and soul.
